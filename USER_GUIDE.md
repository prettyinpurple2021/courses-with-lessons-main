## Understanding Sequential Progression

**Important**: SoloSuccess Intel Academy uses a strict sequential progression system. This means:

### Course Progression
- You start with **Course One: Foundation & Mindset**
- You must complete Course One before accessing Course Two
- Courses unlock in order: 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5 → 6 → 7
- **You cannot skip ahead to later courses**

### Lesson Progression
Each course has 12 lessons that must be completed in order:
- Complete Lesson 1 before accessing Lesson 2
- Complete all 12 lessons before accessing the Final Project
- Locked lessons show a lock icon

### Activity Progression
Each lesson contains multiple activities:
- Activities must be completed sequentially
- Complete Activity 1 before Activity 2, etc.
- All activities must be completed to mark a lesson as complete

### Why Sequential?
This structure ensures you build knowledge systematically and master each concept before moving forward.

## Taking Courses

### Viewing Course Details

1. Click on a course card from your dashboard
2. View the course overview with:
   - Course description
   - Learning outcomes
   - 12 lesson breakdown
   - Prerequisites (if any)

### Starting a Lesson

1. Click on an unlocked lesson
2. The lesson page includes:
   - **Video Player**: YouTube video with custom controls
   - **Note-Taking Panel**: Take notes with timestamps
   - **Activities Section**: Interactive exercises
   - **Resources**: Downloadable materials

### Video Features

- **Play/Pause**: Control video playback
- **Progress Tracking**: Your position is saved automatically
- **Resume**: Pick up where you left off
- **Speed Control**: Adjust playback speed
- **Fullscreen**: Watch in fullscreen mode
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Use spacebar to play/pause

### Taking Notes

1. Click the "Notes" tab while watching
2. Type your notes in the text area
3. Notes auto-save every 30 seconds
4. Click "Add Timestamp" to link notes to video position
5. Review notes anytime from the lesson page

### Downloading Resources

1. Scroll to the "Resources" section
2. Click the download button for any resource
3. Files are saved to your downloads folder

## Completing Activities

### Activity Types

1. **Quiz**: Multiple choice or true/false questions
2. **Exercise**: Practical exercises to apply concepts
3. **Reflection**: Written reflections on what you learned
4. **Practical Task**: Hands-on tasks to complete

### How to Complete Activities

1. Activities appear in sequential order
2. Click on an unlocked activity to start
3. Read the instructions carefully
4. Complete the activity requirements
5. Click "Submit" when finished
6. View feedback (if provided)
7. The next activity unlocks automatically

### Activity Status

- **Unlocked**: Green checkmark, ready to complete
- **Locked**: Lock icon, complete previous activities first
- **Completed**: Holographic checkmark badge

## Final Projects and Exams

### Final Project

After completing all 12 lessons:

1. The Final Project unlocks
2. Read the project requirements carefully
3. Complete the project according to instructions
4. Submit your work through the platform
5. Wait for approval (if required)

### Final Exam

After submitting your Final Project:

1. The Final Exam unlocks
2. Review the exam format and time limit
3. Click "Start Exam" when ready
4. Answer all questions
5. Submit before time expires
6. View your results immediately

### Passing Requirements

- Final Exam passing score varies by course
- You must pass the exam to unlock the next course
- If you don't pass, you can retake the exam

## Earning Certificates

### How to Earn a Certificate

1. Complete all 12 lessons in a course

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I skip ahead to a later course?**
A: No, courses must be completed sequentially to ensure proper knowledge building.

**Q: What happens if I fail the final exam?**
A: You can retake the exam after reviewing the course material.

**Q: How long do I have access to courses?**
A: Lifetime access to all enrolled courses.

**Q: Can I download videos?**
A: Videos are streamed from YouTube and cannot be downloaded, but you can access them anytime.

**Q: Are there deadlines for completing courses?**
A: No deadlines - learn at your own pace.

**Q: Can I pause my enrollment?**
A: Yes, you can take breaks and resume anytime.
